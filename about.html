Cities

Toronto

  • Canada's biggest city
  • Home to over 200 distinct ethnic origins
  • Has the tallest tower in North America(CN Tower)
  • Home to Canada's only NBA team(Raptors) and MLB team(Blue Jays)

Vancouver

  • Canada's 2nd biggest city
  • Ranked as the 5th most liveable city in the world
  • One of the few places in Canada that barely experiencxes snow
  • Lies next to the Pacific Ocean

Montreal

  • Canada's 3rd biggest city
  • One of the oldest cities in Canada
  • North America's largest French speaking city
  • Hosted the first ever olympics in Canada(1976)
  • The bagel was invented here

Calgary

  • Nicknamed Cowtown
  • Ranked as the 4th most liveable city in the world
  • Hosted the winter olympics in 1988
  • Situated in the praries, right next to the Canadian Rockies

Edmonton

  • Home to the world famous, Edmonton Oilers Hockey Club
  • Home to the bhiggest shopping mall in North America(West Edmonton Mall)
  • Has a park system that is 22 times the size of Central Park in New York City(River Valley)
  • Nicknamed Canada's festival city
